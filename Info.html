Font Checker for PowerPoint

Ensure your fonts are installed and correct — before showtime.

✅ What It Does

  • Scans all slides and master layouts in your PowerPoint presentation
  • Lists every font used in slides and slide masters
  • Flags fonts missing from the local machine
  • Displays affected slide numbers for quick fixes
  • Helps avoid font substitution and layout issues

👤 Who It's For

  • Event graphics operators and AV techs
  • Designers handing off decks to clients
  • Presenters using unfamiliar machines

💻 System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or 11
  • Microsoft PowerPoint 365 (desktop)
